INVIEWS

AI-Powered Assessment & Talent Development Platform

AI-led assessments and personalized employee development. Built for large-scale talent evaluation, INVIEWS ensures readiness, maps skills, and drives continuous growth.

What Makes INVIEWS Different?

INVIEWS uses adaptive AI assessments, voice and face analysis, and real-time scoring to go far beyond traditional testing. It connects assessments to actionable development plans.

ProfileAI – Smart Candidate Analysis & Prediction

Education & Academic Strengths: Evaluates academic background, certifications, and learning paths.

Skills Genome Mapping: Identifies core and transferable skills with proficiency levels.

Work Experience Intelligence: Analyzes career progression and domain expertise.

AI-Driven Candidate Scoring: Ranks applicants based on job fit, potential, and culture.

Predictive Hiring Insights: Forecasts long-term success and alignment.
