What is KABi?
KABi is a Saudi-based HR tech company offering AI-powered solutions for hiring, assessment, and career development. Our platform includes HYRDD (AI hiring), INVIEWS (assessment & development), and JOBBIT (career & hiring for SMEs).
Are your platforms subscription-based?
Yes. All KABi solutions operate on flexible subscription models that can be customized to fit organizational size and needs. We offer annual billing options.
Do you provide onboarding or implementation support?
Absolutely. We offer guided onboarding and full implementation support, including platform configuration, admin training, and data migration if needed.
What psychological model do you use for psychometric assessments?
Our psychometric engine is built on the Big Five Personality Model (OCEAN)—a globally recognized framework in organizational psychology.
Are INVIEWS assessments accredited?
Yes. INVIEWS assessments are academically and professionally recognized. We are accredited by London Business School and the Association for Business Psychology (ABP) in the U.S.

Can I customize my hiring stages and workflows in HYRDD?
Yes. HYRDD is fully customizable—clients can create and adjust hiring stages, interview types, scoring methods, and workflows to match internal processes.
How does your AI matching work?
HYRDD uses contextual AI matching, which evaluates candidate profiles beyond just keywords. It considers transferable skills, industry fit, growth potential, and role alignment.
Does HYRDD support automated interviews?
Yes. HYRDD includes AI-powered technical and behavioral interview modules that generate, score, and analyze responses in real-time.
What psychological model do you use for psychometric assessments?

Are INVIEWS assessments accredited?
Yes. INVIEWS assessments are academically and professionally recognized. We are accredited by London Business School and the Association for Business Psychology (ABP) in the U.S.
Can I use INVIEWS for training and development?
Absolutely. INVIEWS generates AI-powered IDPs (Individual Development Plans) and provides personalized training recommendations, including Gen AI-curated learning content and progress tracking.
Is support available 24/7?
Yes. We offer 24/7 support for all enterprise clients through email, live chat, and a dedicated support portal.
Do you offer integration with other HR systems?
Yes. Our platforms integrate with most major HRIS and ATS systems via API or custom connectors.
Where is KABi based?
KABi is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and proudly serves clients across the Kingdom and the Gulf region
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure and comply with local and international data protection standards. All data is encrypted and access-controlled.
Do you comply with Saudi data regulations?
100%. KABi follows all applicable laws and regulations regarding data handling and privacy within Saudi Arabia.
