 KABi on the Global Stage

 We proudly participate in leading HR, AI, and technology events across the region—sharing insights, showcasing innovation, and shaping the future of work alongside industry leaders.

GITEX X KABi

Where Innovation Meets Action!

At GITEX, one of the world’s leading tech events, KABi made a bold statement in the future of work. Our presence attracted decision-makers, tech leaders, and HR professionals eager to explore smarter ways to hire and assess talent. Through live demos, AI showcases, and strategic conversations, we presented how KABi’s solutions are changing the way organizations find and grow talent—faster, smarter, and with data they can trust.

LEAP X KABi

The Future of Talent Starts Here!

At LEAP, surrounded by the world’s brightest minds in technology, KABi stood at the center of innovation in HR. Our booth became a hub for thought-provoking discussions, live product tours, and interactive sessions. We introduced our AI-powered platforms that help companies make better hiring decisions and support employee development. Every visitor walked away with a new perspective on what’s possible in HR tech—and how KABi is leading the change.

LEAP X KABi

Shaping the Next Generation of Talent!

This year also marked a major milestone with the launch of JOBBIT, our new AI-powered career development and hiring platform. Designed to connect job seekers with meaningful opportunities, JOBBIT empowers both individuals and organizations with intelligent tools like career path planning, psychometric assessments, and smart job matching. Whether you're building your first CV or growing a startup team, JOBBIT bridges the gap between talent and opportunity—making the future of work more accessible, personalized, and inclusive for all.

SHRMMENA

Where HR Strategy Meets Smart Technology

KABi joined SHRM MENA to connect with executive HR leaders and share our vision for the future of talent management. We introduced solutions that go beyond automation—combining intelligent recruitment tools with psychometric insights and real-time data. Our conversations focused on real business impact: faster hiring, stronger teams, and smarter growth. The response was clear—HR is ready for a shift, and KABi is at the front of it.
